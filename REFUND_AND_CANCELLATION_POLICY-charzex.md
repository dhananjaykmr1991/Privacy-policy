# Refund and Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines how cancellations and refunds are handled on the Charzex Platform.

---

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancellation requests must be made within **1 day** of placing the order
- Orders already shipped cannot be cancelled
- Customers may reject the order at delivery (if applicable)

---

## Non-Cancellable Items

- Perishable items are non-cancellable
- Refund or replacement may be provided for defective items

---

## Damaged or Defective Products

- Must be reported within **1 day** of delivery
- Subject to verification by the seller

---

## Product Not as Described

- Complaints must be raised within **1 day**
- Resolution will be handled by customer support

---

## Manufacturer Warranty

- Products with manufacturer warranty must be claimed directly with the manufacturer

---

## Refund Timeline

- Approved refunds are processed within **8 days**
